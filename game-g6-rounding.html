How to Play:

Round the number correctly to make your car go faster!

Correct answer = speed boost! Wrong answer = slow down!

How to Round Numbers:

  • Rounding to the nearest ten: Look at the ones digit. If it's 5 or more, round up. If it's 4 or less, round down.
  • Example: 73 rounded to the nearest ten is 70 (because 3 is less than 5)
  • Rounding to the nearest hundred: Look at the tens digit. If it's 5 or more, round up. If it's 4 or less, round down.
  • Example: 486 rounded to the nearest hundred is 500 (because 8 is more than 5)
  • Rounding to the nearest thousand: Look at the hundreds digit. If it's 5 or more, round up. If it's 4 or less, round down.
  • Example: 3,427 rounded to the nearest thousand is 3,000 (because 4 is less than 5)
